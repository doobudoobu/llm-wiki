User wants to explore the parallel between Hegel's *Sittlichkeit* / Spirit's self-withdrawal and the Chinese Mandate of Heaven (*Tian ming* 天命) as structurally analogous frameworks for legitimate authority.

**Why:** Both claim that authority is not merely imposed from outside but is the *inner* logic of institutional legitimacy — and both describe its collapse not as external defeat but as the rational principle withdrawing from a form that has failed to embody it.
